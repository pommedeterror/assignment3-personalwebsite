​ ​ ​ Spider-Monkey

Plot

Xyliana, a divine vessel, travels the world of Midiron in order to collect lost pieces of her soul. Each of the 4 kingdoms of Midiron are themed off of a category of nature.

Characters

Xyliana

Xyliana is a divine vessel, and one of the five beings that survived Midiron’s extinction event. She’s the most powerful humanoid creature on Midiron and messing with her is almost guaranteed death.
She’s quite forgetful.

Kenji

Kenji is a runaway from Soraria that joins Xyliana through the bulk of her journey. He's a soft spoken, sheltered genius with some self esteem issues. He has a special interest in Zarez Mana, the insect kingdom.

Xiareth

Xiareth is a historical figure Xyliana blessed personally. He’s remembered as the Great Emperor of Soraria to some, the Dread Dictator of Midiron to most. He basically invented colonization. Yahoo!

Kyros

Kyros is a knight and served Xiareth. He was known as his blade, mostly because he led so many bloody conquests. His wings were cut off in order to shape him into something less “beastly.” It’s a process most Sorarians went through during Xiareth’s reign.

Suma

Suma is a revolutionary, trying to undo the damage Soraria’s done to Midiron (which is a lot). She’s pretty tired of all the sh*t she’s witnessed throughout the years though, and struggles to see herself as an actual person rather than a cog in the revolution’s machine.

Kirran

Kirran is the prince of the Underground Kingdom: Zarez Mana. He’s very friendly, and always looking to learn something new about the outside world. He’s never seen the sky but he’d do anything to catch a glimpse of it. And I mean anything. Otherwise, he’s weirdly normal.

Nexus

Nexus is the child god that resides in the void. They’re the source of all magic in Midiron, as well as the sun, the moon, the sky, and its impending doom.
