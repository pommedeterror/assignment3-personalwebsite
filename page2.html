Plot

Heraverse is an urban fantasy political drama that takes place in Japan. The story details the conflict between the following families: the Hoshino Clan, the Kamadou Clan, and the Gojo Clan. This used to be a Jujutsu Kaisen AU. The difference is that this one is better.

Characters

Hera Hoshino

Hera is a CEO, and the titular character of Heraverse. Her company specializes in shipping and cursed weapons manufacturing. She’s the first protagonist of Heraverse, as well as its second villain. She murdered her uncle for the company and is currently killing the rest of the Kamadou clan to maintain power.

Cousin Hoshino

Cousin is the second protagonist. Also goes by "Itoko", and, later, Aeneas Bellerose. He and Hera are cousins, and Hera didn’t care about them enough to put an actual name on their adoption forms. They used to be the heir to the Hoshino clan before Hera killed his father. They’re illiterate, super into visual kei, and hungry for revenge.
