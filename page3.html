Plot

A Chinese-American spin on the classic Spider-man formula. This Peter— Peter Ke– is an aspiring CS student that got bit by a radioactive spider monkey, and got the regular Spiderman powers along with extra hair and a tail.

He lives in Shanghattan, the busiest, most tech-advanced city in the world. The beginning of SpiderMonkey, as a vigilante, happens almost by accident, as he attempts to save his school festival from a rogue government mecha.

Characters

Peter Ke

Peter Ke is this Earth’s Peter Parker. He was very standoffish before the radioactive bite, and had difficulty connecting with others, opting instead to shut himself in his room and study or tinker around with robotics.

Marissa Jang

Marissa Jang is this Earth’s MJ, and she has the personality to back it up. Assertive, ambitious, and socially competent, she will do anything to become the greatest news reporter Shanghattan has ever seen.

Benjamin Ke

This Earth's Uncle Ben goes by the name Uncle Benny (or Benny Shushu). He is a father figure to Peter, as Peter's actual father fails to provide the proper emotional bond an adolescent boy needs. He has lost touch with him in recent years, though.
